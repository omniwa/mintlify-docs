<div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (120).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

### My Dashboard

My Dashboard is the personal landing page for each user. The exact widgets shown depend on the user’s role and the modules they have access to. Refer to the corresponding chapter (Compliance, Performance, Investment, Dealing, etc.) for details about the data feeding the dashboard.



### Change Password
